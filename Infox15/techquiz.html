TECH-QUIZ

  • Event Details
  • Register

Come, participate and prove your technical knowledge in InfoX's event, TechQuiz in which teams compete against one another in an effort to prove themselves as the best and the brightest.

 

Details

There are three rounds in the competition

 

First Round

This round will be conducted online. Registered participants shall receive the link for the quiz via email. The quiz will be of 20 minutes and will consist of 30 questions.

  1. You can take this test anytime between the start date/time and end date/time.
  2. Once the test starts, you cannot pause it, the test will run continuously for 20 Minutes.
  3. Please check that your system is compatible before starting test. Use this link: http://www.hackerearth.com/system-check/.

 

On completion of the online quiz, all teams will be ranked on the basis of their score and the top ten teams shall proceed to the next round.

 

Second Round

Top 10 Teams from round 1, shall proceed to the second round which will be conducted on 13th March 2015. Eligible Teams will be notified of the event details via email.

 

Third Round

Top 5 teams from round 2 shall compete in round 3, which will be held on 13th March 2015. Eligible teams will be notified of the details on the spot.

 

Eligibility

Open to all

 

Number of Participants per Team

1-2

 

Rules

  1. The competition is open to students of all colleges across India.
  2. Students are allowed participate in groups as well as individually.
  3. Each team is allowed a single attempt in all rounds. Multiple attempts shall lead to disqualification.

 

Important Dates

  1. 12th March 2015 – Last date for first round
  2. 13th March 2015 – Date for Second and Third Routine
