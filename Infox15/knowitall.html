KNOW IT ALL


“If you think you know it all, think again”

 

Synopsis:

  1. Only two members are allowed in a team.
  2. The competition will consist of three rounds (and a tie breaker if needed).
  3. The first round will be objective type with questions of aptitude, quantitative analysis and general reasoning.
  4. The second round will also be objective type consisting of questions related to G.K. and G.S. and
  5. The last round will be descriptive type (most likely a questionnaire or picture description). The tie breaker will be ad hoc if required.
  6. The students are required to bring their own stationary (pen, pencils, etc.).
  7. The combined score of both the students will be considered for each round.
  8. The event will require a classroom with a projector and an extra classroom as per the participation.
  9. All rounds will take 15 min for completion and 15 min for evaluation.
  10. Print outs of question papers and answer sheets, along with some rough sheets will be used.
