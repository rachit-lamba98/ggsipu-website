ALMIGHTEE


Find the creative genius within you here at InfoX. If you are a graphic designer and wish for your work to be displayed before the world, we offer you a platform to do this. Come up with a T-shirt design which is innovative, unique and above all, awesome. Share your design and if chosen the best by public consensus, it’ll be the design for the official InfoXpression T-shirt. Prove that you are AlmighTEE.


General Rules:

  1. The competition is open to students of all colleges across India.
  2. Every participant can submit a maximum of one entry.
  3. The Winning Design will be used as the official  InfoXpression T-Shirts.
  4. Participants must ensure the following while designing the T-Shirts:
    1. The design must be wholly original.
    2. The design must not incorporate or include any material that would require the consent of a third part.
    3. The design must not violate any copyright, trademark, publicity right, privacy right, or any other right of any third party.
    4. The design must not include offensive language, imagery or themes.
    5. The design must not feature, reference or contain tobacco, alcohol, drugs, rebel flags, sexual connotations.
    6. The InfoXpression Organizing Committee reserves the right to make minor adjustments or alterations to the winning design.
    7. We are providing a starter kit to aid participants in designing the T-Shirts. The starter kit contains base T-Shirts for designing and detailed rules regarding the design specifications. It can be downloaded at the following link: http://goo.gl/e2oX0D

Submission of Entries:

Participants should send in their entries via email at infox.tee@gmail.com along with the following details:

  1. Name of Participant
  2. Name of College
  3. Course and Year
  4. Contact Number
  5. E-mail ID
  6. Additional details involving tools used for designing and design inspiration

Design Related Rules:

  1. The design must be in .png/.ai/.pdf/.jpg format. It is best if your design is created in Photoshop/Illustrator or another professional design program.
  2. The base colour of the T-Shirt must be one of the following:
    1. Black
    2. White
    3. Navy Blue
    4. Royal Blue
    5. Red
    6. Grey
    7. For detailed design related rules read the PDF file titled “Designing Guidelines” in our starter kit.

Judging Criteria:

  1. The entries will be judged on the basis of likes and shares on the facebook page.
  2. It is mandatory for all voters to have liked the InfoXpression facebook page before liking any design.
  3. Only the likes on the photos on the InfoXpression facebook page will be counted.
  4. Automatically generated likes of any kind will lead to disqualification.
  5. The top 5 designs in terms of likes and shares will be selected at the end of the deadline.
  6. The InfoXpression team will then choose the winner out of the selected 5 T-Shirt Designs.
  7. The winning design will be chosen on the basis of the following:
    1. Concept
    2. Visual value
    3. Imaginative merit
    4. Innovation and Originality
    5. Entertainment value.

Important Deadlines:

  1. 7th March, 2015: Last date for submission of entries and liking on facebook.
