Programme: M.Ed.

Programme Duration:

  • It is a one-year ( Two Semesters ) Masters Programme that would culminate in PG Degree, i.e., M.Ed.
  • The M.Ed Programme has been approved by the NCTE.

THE DESIGN OF THE COURSE CONTENT FOR THEORY PAPERS:

The course content for each paper would comprise four inter-related units.

PROGRAMME TRANSACTION

The M.Ed Programme involves lectures, discussions, practicum, sessional tasks and seminars.
