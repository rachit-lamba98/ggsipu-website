Teaching and Research Experience:

Dr. Anjali Shokeen, Presently working as Assistant Professor at University School of Education, GGSIP University. She has been a teacher educator for 9 years. She obtained her M.Com, B.Ed, M.Ed, MBA from M.D. University Rohtak and Ph.D (Education) from Jamia Milla Islamia, New Delhi. Before joining University School of Education, GGSIPU, she worked as an Assistant Professor in R.C. Institute of Technology, Najafgarh (Affiliated to GGSIPU) for 8 years (from august 2005 to December 2013). She worked as a staff coordinator in B.Ed Programme of IGNOU.

She is empanelled as an Academic counselor in B.Ed Programme of IGNOU, study centre 0781 from 2005 and for M.A. Education since 2008. She has been invited as Resource person in different institutions.
