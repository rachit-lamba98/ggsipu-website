Teaching & Research Experience

Dr Dhananjay Joshi,Presently Assocaite Professor, University School of Education, GGSIPU University , Delhi obtained his M.A (Political Science), B.Ed. & M.Ed. degree with gold medal and Ph.D. (education) from university of Delhi . He has been teaching education to both Under Graduate and Post Graduate students for the last 12years. Prior to joining University School of Education, GGSIPU, he was with Maharaja Surajmal Institute, Janakpuri, Delhi (Affiliated to GGSIPU) for 8 years (December 2000 to October 2008). He has worked as project fellow in Maulana Azad Center for Elementary Education (MACESE), University of Delhi (1997 to 1998). His areas of academic and research include Curriculum Development, Teacher education and value education etc. He was awarded prestigious Radha Krishnan Memorial award 2008 in field of education. He is on the panel of Northern regional Committee (NCTE) Jaipur. He has authored two books on education and written many valuable articles pertaining to educational and social issues in the referred journals, Magazines and Newspapers.
