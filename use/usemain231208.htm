School's Vision

  • Embodying Gandhi's dictum, “Let us become the change we seek in this world,” the school would provide an innovative but inclusive pedagogical space for the creation of a new system of education.
  • It will create a space for free and fearless but socially sensitive inquiry and research geared towards human dignity and social responsibility.

School's Mission

  “Let noble thoughts come from all around.”

Our school of education shall be centre of learning, it will empower the students, create passion for research and development, help them and imbibe the spirit of accepting the new challenges and be committed to the overall cause of education.
