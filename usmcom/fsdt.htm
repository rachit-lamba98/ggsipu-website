Dr. Sarvesh Dutt Tripathi, Assistant Professor
  
         
 


Course Coordinator
MMC

 

After the completion of Master in Journalism and Mass Communication, Dr. Tripathi served in different Hindi and English newspapers such as The Hindustan Times and Amar Ujala in various capacities. In 2002, he joined MBICEM, one of the affiliated colleges of G.G.S.I.P University as a Lecturer. He worked in VIPS, another affiliated college of the University from 2003 to 2008. He joined then Centre for Media Studies, GGSIP University in 2008. He has successfully completed two refresher courses organised by University Grants Commission and is a life member of American Studies Research Circle , Hyderabad . During his tenure as a Correspondent in The Hindustan Times, Lucknow and earlier as Bureau Chief, Amar Ujala, he also worked as State Secretary of People's Union for Civil Liberties from 1990 to 2002, worked as Course Coordinator of Journalism and Mass Communication, PG Diploma Course, conducted by Purvanchal University , Jaunpur.
