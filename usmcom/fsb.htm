Sachin Bharti, Assistant Professor
  
         
 

 

Mr. Bharti has done his MA (Mass Communication) with first division from A.J.K. Mass Communication Research Centre, Jamia Millia Islamia University, Delhi in 2004.  Thereafter, he joined Asian School of Media Studies as Lecturer and H.O.D. Mass Communication.  He has also worked with National Institute of Fashion Technology, New Delhi, as Assistant Professor.  He joined University School of Mass Communication (the then University Centre for Media Studies), GGSIP University in December 2008.

Sachin Bharti is an awardee of National Scholarship in Theater, Ministry of Culture, Government of India.  He is also pursuing Ph. D on the topic “Changing Aspects of Hindi Cinema & Its Impact on Society.”  He is also editing a book on Film & Video Editing: Theory & Practical.

He has publised research papers in various research journals of repute.
