Sweta Singh, Assistant Professor
  
         
 

 

Ms. Singh joined the then University Centre for Media Studies in November 2008. She has been the Course Coordinator for Post-graduate Diploma in Television Journalism at Apeejay Institute of Mass Communication, New Delhi . She has a teaching experience of over eight years. Before joining academics, she worked as Reporter, Doordarshan News, New Delhi .

She has published research papers in reputed national and international journals. Besides, she has been presenting presentation papers in nations conferences in India & abroad. Her areas of specialisation include Television Journalism, New and Alternative Media. She has done her Masters in Mass Communication from Guru Jambeshwar University , Hissar and Post-graduate Diploma in Radio and Television Journalism from Indian Institute of Mass Communication, New Delhi.
