Vinay Shanker, Assistant Professor
  
         
 

Additional Coordinator
MMC

Coordinator
MJMC

  

Mr. Vinay Shanker has been working as a Media Professional since 1995. He has primarily worked as a Cinematographer though his work profile also includes credits as a Director, Script Writer and Photographer. He has worked for organisations like Star Plus, National Geographic Channel and Priyardarshini Media. He has shot and directed eight serials and several short programmes for Doordarshan, ETV Urdu, Gyan Darshan, Sahitya Academy etc. His documentary work also includes a film Nirmal Kathi (Script, Cinematography and Direction) on renowned Hindi Writer Nirmal Verma. ‘PHALI’ (cinematography), a film on the impact of insurgency on women in Manipur, has been shown widely in various film festivals across Europe.

He has done his MA (Mass Communications) from the MCRC, Jamia Millia Islamia. He taught as Lecturer (Video Production) at MBICEM affiliated to GGSIPU from 2002 to 2006. He also helped to establish India's first Student Film Festival called “First Frame”. He worked as a Lecturer in Department of Mass Media and Mass Communications in Indraprastha College for Women, Delhi University from 2006 to 2008. Besides teaching in the main course, he also coordinated the add-on course in Anchoring, News Reading and Video Production offered by the College. He has conducted workshops in Cinematography, Lighting and Cinema appreciation for students and faculty of various institutions. He joined the then University Centre for Media Studies, GGSIPU, in October, 2008.

Interest Areas
Cinema and Urbanity in India, Cinema and Space, Developments in Cinematography in India.
