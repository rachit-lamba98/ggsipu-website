Prof. C. P. Singh
  
         
 


Professor
USMC

 


  

Prof. Singh joined as Professor at USMC in May 2010. He is a bilingual educator, author and researcher with over 21 years of industry, academic and administrative experience and has been associated with the Bennett & Coleman Co.(Delhi & Patna), Fairfax Business Media (Singapore), Discovery Channel(Delhi), IIMC (Delhi) and Jagran Institute of Management and Mass Communication (NOIDA).

His publications include:

  • Before the Headlines: A Handbook of Television Journalism. Macmillan. Delhi.
  • News on the Web, News for the Web in Making News (Ed.Uday  Sahay), Oxford University Press. Delhi.
  • Discovery Channel Hindi Style Book (Member, Core Team). Delhi.
  • Dictionary of Media and Journalism. IK International. Delhi.
  • MIS Stargazer  (ed.), Strategic Publishing Group. Singapore.

He has been invited to national and international seminars/conferences held by institutions such as Gartner Inc., University of Hyderabad, UNESCO and IGNOU; and, has to his credit more than a hundred case studies, research papers, reports, articles, reviews and TV scripts written for various leading magazines, journals, newspapers, websites and TV channels.

A member of European Communication Research and Education Association (ECREA), Belgium, & International Association for Media and Communication Research (IAMCR), UK; on the boards of various academic institutions including NCERT (Text Book Committee on Media Studies), he has been guide to over half a dozen Ph. D. scholars.
 
Interest Areas
Designing Strategic Syllabi; Media Education Models; Media Economics; Communication of Innovations for Freedom; News Content & Language Trends.
