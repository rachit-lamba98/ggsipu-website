TEACHING/ RESEARCH EXPERIENCE: 

Dr. Anand P. Singh’s current interests are in the area of library automation, networking, web based information resources, open access archives and systems. He obtained his Bachelor’s degree (B.Sc.) (PCM) in 1987 from Agra University, Agra; B.Lib.I.Sc. degree in 1991 from Dr H S Gaur University, Sagar and M.Lib.I.Sc. degree in 1992 from Jiwaji University, Gwalior. He worked at various premiere and national research organizations viz, Indira Gandhi National Centre for the Arts (IGNCA), Centre for Biomedical Engineering (CBME), Central Library Indian Institute of Technology (IIT, Delhi), and Indian Agricultural Research Institute (IARI) Library from where he has joined Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi in 2004 as Deputy Librarian. For a short while, he was gone for teaching assignment as Reader, Library and Information Science, Bundelkhand University, Jhansi. He obtained his Ph.D. in 1997 from the School of Library and Information Science, Jiwaji University, Gwalior, for his work on ‘An investigation into the development of computer-based multimedia information system with special reference to voice data’. He was visiting guest faculty, paper setter and examiner for various universities, deemed universities and state agricultural universities. He has held mission-mode project from ICAR-NATP under financial assistance of World Bank as Co-PI.

Presently, he is looking after Online Subscriptions of e-journals and databases, Library Automation, Networking, UGC-INFONET e-Consortium, Maintenance of UIRC Webpage, Computer Center, Audio Visual Unit, Orientation Programme, Off Campus Access, E-Prints and Electronic Thesis and Dissertation (ETD) of GGS Indraprastha University.
