UIRC has subscribed Turnitin - Cloud based Anti Plagiarism Software for 2017-18 with 10 Instructors and 100 Students Licenses in view of “Maintenance of Academics Values and Ethical Research Standards Regulation 2013” to prevent plagiarism.

 

Turnitin allows each Instructor to create up to 10 (Student) accounts for fellow faculty members of their respective schools "for submitting the documents for Plagiarism Detection / Originality Check / Similarity Analysis".
