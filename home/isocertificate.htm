ISO 9001:2000 Certification

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University has been awarded the ISO 9001:2000 Certification by Standardization, Testing and Quality Certification Directorate, Department of Information Technology, Ministry of Communication and Information Technology, Government of India, for a period of three years.

The University has been awarded the Certificate of Registration bearing No. SQ/ ISO/9001/584 in recognition of its establishment and maintenance of Quality Management System, which compiles with the requirements of ISO 9001:2000. It is pertinent to mention here that the scope of this Certificate of Registration covers the design, development and delivery of professional educational programmes including consultancy projects in the areas of Biotechnology, Chemical Technology, Environment Management, Humanities and Social Sciences, Information Technology, Law & Legal Studies, Management Sciences, and Basic & Applied Sciences.
