About M.Tech. (Engineering Physics)

 

M.Tech. (EP) is the first ever course at post-graduate level in India. The programme is planned in such a manner so as to bridge the gap between Engineering and Science streams. It meets the demands of the technical manpower required in several new emerging areas. This course prepare students to work in the Industries/Institues in the field of : Solar photovoltaics, Nuclear Energy Technologies, Nano Science and Technology, Embedded Systems, Computational simulations, visualization technology, networking. The Engineering Physics course at undergraduate level has already been started at the institutes like IIT’s, DCE (changed to DTU) etc.

  • It is interdisciplinary and has courses from various fields of applied sciences and computer sciences. In Applied Sciences some of the focus areas are Photovoltaics, Embedded systems, Computational Physics, Nanoscience and Technology, Ion Beam Technology, Photonics.

  • In computational sciences courses on programming (C++, Java, Oracle etc.), networking, data base management systems, computer architecture, multimedia and visualization technology etc. are covered.

Highlights of the programme:

  • The programme is approved by AICTE.

  • Regular interactive sessions with eminent physicists and physicist turned entrepreneur/manager/administrator as part of the curriculum.

  • Presentations cum Comprehensive Viva-Voce in each semester

  • Project work by actual participation in Industry/ national research laboratories like SSPL, LASTEK, NPL, IUAC, VECC, CDAC etc.

  • Working in the ongoing research projects at USS

  • Visit to Industry/Research labs

  • Students attend the national and international level conferences organized by  FICCI, ASSOCHAM, SESI, EI group and various universities/institutes/ organizations.
