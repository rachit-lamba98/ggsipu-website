GIRLS HOSTEL

The University has recently opened a hostel for girls which provides excellent residential facilities . It is located in the campus and is open for all categories of students , but the preference is given to the outside Delhi students.

The University charges the students a very nominal fees for this Hostel facility and provides all of them with extremely comfortable and safe rooms. This newly provided hostel Facility has not only helped the girls of The University to inter mingle among each other in a better way but has also given them a chance to access the University Library and Laboratories for a longer period of time. This Hostel has been a great comfort to outside Delhi girls as a large part of there day after University was otherwise spend in commuting.   The Hostel mess is run by students. The food charges depend upon the number of students residing in the hostel and is collected by the hostel management committee.

Note: Hostel will be available only to the bonafide students of University School of Studies and IGIT
