PLAY GROUND
(Facilities)

The University Campus also gives the students the facility of several grounds so that they can play and enjoy themselves in free time.

It has a basket ball court just near it canteen where a regular crowd of basket ball lovers both players and spectators are found daily testing their skill and competing with each other. A part of the main ground in the campus has Ben made into the volley ball court where irrespective of the heat rain or cold a regular crowd of students playing this game is always found. The main ground is also large enough to be used as a field for some very frequent and some relay competitive football matches. And of course during rainy days it is used to play the very amazing and interesting Rugby matches.

But of course like all of India, the University students main calling in the field of games is Cricket. Spending hours and hours in some very nail biting matches is a common time pass of our University students. Not only inter class but inter year inter University schools but also inter college competitions are held on a regular basis.

So the University is a not only a center of academic work but also gives the students ample opportunity to test their skills and make themselves physically strong by playing.
