BOYS HOSTEL

University has opened a hostel for boys to provide students the conducive ambience for learning & leading a balance life. While all the regular students of GGSIPU are eligible for hostel accommodation, preference will be given to outside Delhi students. The hostel has given students a platform to interact after hectic college hours. The students also get the benefit of accessing the university information resources centre and university laboratories to maximum, which would have been spend commuting concurrently. A cooperative mess, run by the students, provides inmates to decide among themselves the kind of food that suits their appetite. Proper attention is given for the hygiene and cleanliness of the hostel, and all the residents are expected to keep their rooms neat and tidy. A back-up generator is also provided for round the clock power supply.

Facilities :

  • Boys hostel also provides  a T.V room and a Reading Room for the recreational activities.

  • It has also a badminton Court and Table Tennis Table within the hostel, other sports items also available to the inmate like basket ball, volley ball, badminton, etc.

  • In addition to the sports equipments and items ,it also has its own gymnasium for the boys.

  • In case of an illness/injury ,First Aid Box is available in the hostel.
