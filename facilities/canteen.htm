CANTEEN
(Facilities)

In order to satisfy the appetites of the students and to give them a break after a half day's hard work the University campus is provided with a relaxing and comfortable canteen. The canteen not only provides the student's of the campus with mouth watering dishes like 'Sambhar-Vada', Sambhar-Idli', 'Rajma-Chawal', 'Uttapam','Fried-Rice', 'Spring Rolls','Thali' etc. but is also one of the most happening places in the campus with all the students gathering there at the clock's stroke of 1 o' clock to discuss their new experiences in the campus. The canteen provided by Handa Caterers has been sponsored by Pepsi and is usually the place of daily normal hob nob of the students and generally the center of attraction during cricket matches and any other live telecasts on television.
