Welcome

The Centre  has been established for the purpose of developing the skills without adequate emphasis on inculcating a strong ethical commitment among students deserves careful consideration and action by policy maker and practitioners in institutions of Education.

Objective

Centre play a positive role in encouraging students and other participants to rise above differences in rites, rituals and modes of prayer adopted by people of different faiths and commit themselves to the core values of humanism, thereby becoming better human being who can achieve richer fulfillment in their own personal and professional lives and effectively spreed the positive message of human and compassionate behaviour in their respective spheres of influence with the family, society, the nation and beyond.
