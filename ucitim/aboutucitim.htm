About UITS Cell...

The University IT Services (UITS) Cell is managing the activities related to maintenance of IT peripherals, Websites, Internet & Intranet, Antivirus, Printer & others.

It is responsible the following activities : -

  1. To examine the proposal of various departments and administration for procurement of Computers and peripherals (Technical Review).
  2. Maintenance of all the Computers, Peripherals and networking including their AMC
  3. University Internet & Intranet activities
  4. University Website activities
  5. Antivirus Services
  6. Implementation of Digital University Project.
  7. Help Desk / Complaints
