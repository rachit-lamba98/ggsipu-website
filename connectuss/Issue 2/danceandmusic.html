Swarmudra-Dance and Music Society

By Anukriti Jain(USICT)


" An art is a moral law. It gives soul to the universe ,wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and charm and gaiety to life and everything."

It is good to be associated with some kind of art whether it be music or dance

It is always pleasant to be associated with some kind of art, be it music or dance. Swarmudra, the Music and Dance Society of USS is a platform where students can develop this association. The society invites students to join them as they begin preparations for Anugoonj 2014.

The dance society of Swarmudra had its orientation and formal auditions earlier this year. The society organized a dance fiesta on Diwali and is now gearing up for various competitions in Anugoonj like Footloose, Street Dance, Folk Dance etc. Simultaneously, the music society of Swarmuda feautured in HTCity Highlight and performed in the TEDx event. The photos of these events offer a glimpse into the efforts the members are making to bring laurels to the university.

You too can fill the stage with emotions, joy and elation by being a part of the Swarmudra family. Students who wish to join the society can contact the following student coordinators

Music-Rashmi(8802549569)
