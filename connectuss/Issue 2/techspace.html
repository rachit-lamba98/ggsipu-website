Techspace - Electrotech organizes Robotics Workshop

By Anukriti Jain(USICT)



  
"Learning is the only thing the mind never exhausts,never fears and never regret"

Students of our University are constantly making an effort to embrace learning and thus, to proceed further up the tree of knowledge. One such endeavour is Techspace, the technical society of our University. Students of USICT are working every hour to take this society to a whole new level. Electrotech, one of the clubs under Techspace, recently organised many workshops for students.

One of the workshops involving embedded systems was to study the concept of a water level indicator. Students were given ann introductory presentation and further concepts were explained using breadboards. A working model of the system was tested using a water bottle and a buzzer. The workshop was conducted by Lakshay Chutani, Amarjit Singh, Anmol Singh and Sajeet Sah, all 3rd year Electronics and Communication Engineering students.

All the participating students were very happy with the workshop and expressed their wishes of attending more such events in the future. The brilliant reviews clearly indicate that Techspace is truly on an upward trajectory.
