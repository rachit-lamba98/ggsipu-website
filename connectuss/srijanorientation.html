SRIJAN ORIENTATION 2K16

Sanjna Singh (USLLS)

The afternoon of 30th August 2016, couldn’t have been well spent by the fresh lot of USS, as Srijan- Indraprastha Society for Creative Expressions and it’s Club each gave a glimpse of their past and what is about to come. A total of 11 Clubs came forward to encourage and enlighten the new students to join and become a part of the Srijan Society, to establish their college’s name not only in the curricular field but also in the extra-curricular platform. Having won accolades in various competitions, the event was given a melodious beginning by the members of the SwarMudra Club, who through their music and dance had everyone in the auditorium humming and grooving to their tunes. Further, the Constitutional Club, Literary Club, Fine Arts Club, Science Club, Photography Club introduced and talked about their plans for the upcoming year.

Next up, the Publication Club, threw light on the role they played in covering and reporting, each and every event of the Sports Meet and the Annual Cultural Fest, Anugoonj to the faculty and students and expressed their desire for not only passionate and dedicated writers but photographers, web designers, and cartoonists. The Dramatics Club, Aveksha, one of the most active and oldest running club, through its many members that were on the stage showed that, those once in Aveksha, were always Aveksha. They talked about their past productions, accolades and their plans for the year.

This year a new club was introduced by Srijan, The Gender Champions, where the members talked about the current situation of gender inequality and the need to bring about a change. The Corporate Social Responsibility Club that is Enactus presented their agenda and convinced students for participation.
