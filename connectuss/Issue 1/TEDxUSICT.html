Anukriti (USICT)

  
The students of USICT are organizing a TEDx event on the 17th of November, 2014 in the University Campus. TED is a global community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world. These people passionately believe in power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and finally the world. TEDx programs help communities, organizations and individuals to spark conversations and connections through local TED like experiences. The objective of the event is to nurture this ideology and to provide a platform for a plethora of ideas. Speakers from various spheres are invited to come and share their views in this event where visionaries, innovators and thinkers will come together to capture the essence of positive change.
