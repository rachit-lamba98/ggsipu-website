USMC Bestowed With Prestigious B-award

Mehak (USMC)

  
University School of Mass Communication was conferred with the prestigious DNA-STAR Industry Innovative B-School Leadership Award-2014 for innovation, leadership, development and industry interface in a grand ceremony on 17.02.2014 at the hotel Taj Lands End, Bandra, Mumbai.

In the award function VCs of many central and state universities namely: Central University of Jharkhand, Central University of Gujarat, Pondicherry University, Himachal Pradesh University, along with directors/ head of prestigious management and engineering institutions/schools/faculties/departments such as IIM-Rohtak, IBS-Mumbai and University of Hyderabad (school of management studies) remained present to receive the award for their respective institution.
