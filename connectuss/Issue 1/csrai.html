Ques.
As the Director of Student Welfare of GGSIPU, where do you see our university in the next 10 years?
Ans. Our prime focus is to see what the shortcomings in our university are, and where we are lacking. Once that is established, we will work on these issues order to match the level of top universities of India.

Ques.
What are the reforms that you would like to make as the dean of USICT?
Ans.
The main focus right now is to improve the quality of the labs in the school. We are planning to bring some new equipment and try to upgrade the labs.

Ques.

Ans.

Ques.
Are there any plans for new schools/courses to be introduced in the university?
Ans.
Currently, we are not planning to introduce any new school in the university but surely we will introduce some courses like M.Tech. in robotics etc., so that the students have a broader range of options.

Ques.
Where, according to you, are we lacking in comparison to other institutes?
Ans.
We have shifted to this campus just 4 years ago, so we are taking time to provide proper facilities, establish labs and create a friendly environment for studies.

Ques.
What is your message to the students of the university?
