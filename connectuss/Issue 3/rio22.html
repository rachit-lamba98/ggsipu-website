Join Rio+22 Power India Certification

(Short Term Certification Course Study Program)

Suman Saurabh

IARC’s Centre for United Nations is a major group organization with the United Nations. It drives United Nations’ initiatives through national-level certification programmes including the premiere Rio India Programs (2012-2022) for colleges and universities. They enjoy in-kind support of eminent local and foreign organizations, education groups and government offices. Rio India is hailed for its content and approach by the media, authorities, academics and students alike. The testimonials from across India underline the success of the programmes.

The organization is looking for enterprising students in colleges and universities across India with a knack for people interactions and leadership aspirations. The core function is to effectively engage with students on campus and drive participation in Rio+22 Power India Certification Programme. The exam will be conducted offline/online in Feb-Mar 2015 – Opinion descriptive questions only (i.e. no right/wrong answers)

Participants will enjoy the following benefits:
- Certificates with official logos of United Nations SE4ALL and IARC Centre for UN
- Improve your CV by scoring good grades in India’s most prestigious UN initiatives related program
- Special edition Medallions will be awarded to one winner per college
- Win prestigious national recognition in the national hall of fame (22 national level winners)
- Win prizes and more

The details regarding registration in the event are as follows:
Last date of registration: 25th January
Program is available: To students from all streams
Fee & Course Material: 599 per student, Participants get a high quality illustrated book, CD with videos, Wristband
