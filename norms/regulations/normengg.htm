Built Space (Academic)

 

a)       Class Rooms

 

 

Carpet area @ 1.3 sqm carpet area per student in a class

 

 

         There should be one classroom per discipline.

         For the First Year of all disciplines, there should be 2 additional tutorial rooms.

         The distance between the blackboard and the first row of students should not be less than 2.5 meters (8 ft).

         Blackboards should be of adequate size and of good quality (made of glass or plastic coated)

         Lighting and fans should be adequate and in working condition.

 

 

b)       Drawing Halls/Studios

 

Carpet area @ 2.6 sqm carpet area per student for drawing class of 30 students

         One drawing hall required for First year batch

         The distance between the blackboard and the first row of students should not be less than 3 meters (about 10 ft).

         Blackboards should be of adequate size and of good quality (made of glass or plastic coated)

         Lighting and fans should be adequate and in working condition.

 

c)       Central Library

Carpet area @ 0.5 sqm per student in the Institution

         Reading room and stack area should be separated from each other

         Lighting and fans should be adequate and in working condition.

 

d)       Computer Center

 

100 sqm for a batch of 20 students

         There should be glass cabin for computer center staff.

         The Center should be well lighted and air- conditioned.

         UPS should be located outside the Center.

 

e)       Workshops

 

Basic Mechanical Workshop & Material Stores

 

 

 

A total carpet area of

900 sq. meter

         Shops corresponding to the First Year curricula should be available.

         Shops should be well lighted.

         Sufficient exhaust fans should be provided in all shops.

         Safe drinking water and toilet facilities (separate for male and female students) should be provided in the workshop complex.

         Waste disposal must be environment friendly.

         All shops must have updated first aid boxes.

         All foremen should be tested for skill in giving first aid.

         All shops and stores must have at least 2 sets of appropriate type of fire extinguishing equipment. Expiry of these may be checked.
