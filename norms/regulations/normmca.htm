6.

Built Space (Academic)

 

a)  Class Rooms

 

 

 

Carpet area @ 1.3 sqm carpet area per student both for class rooms and tutorial rooms

 

 

·         There should be one classroom and 2 tutorial rooms.

·         The distance between the blackboard and the first row of students should not be less than 3 meters (about 10 ft.)

·         Blackboards should be of adequate size and of good quality (made of glass or plastic coated)

·         Lighting and fans should be adequate and in working conditions

·         Audio-visual equipment such as OHP, slide projector, TV, VCP/VCR, VCD player, etc. should be permanently installed in the classroom.

 

b)   Central Library

Carpet area of 100 sqm.

·         Reading room and stack area should be separated from each other

·         Lighting and fans should be adequate and in working condition.

 

 

c)   Computer Centre

100 sqn carpet area during the First Year

·         There should be glass cabin for computer centre staff.

·         The Center should be well lighted and air-conditioned.

·         UPS should be located outside the Center.
